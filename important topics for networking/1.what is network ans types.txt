Network: A network is a group of devices connected together so they can communicate 
        and share data.
Types of network:
1.LAN: It is local area network.
2.PAN: It is a personal area network.
3.WAN: It is wide area network.
4.MAN: It is Metropolitan area network.

Types of network Architecture: There are two types of network.
1.peer to peer
2.client server network
